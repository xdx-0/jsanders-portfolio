Music Shows Every Friday and Saturday!

Come see our musical shows performed every Weekend. You will see the best in local talent. Zydeco country jazz and blues. We have a special surprise for you every week. You will love the atmosphere in our music stage. We work hand in hand with the jolly inn and other entertainment areas to find for you the best in local entertainment.

Ragin Cajun Chicken Sandwich

You have got to try the Rajun Cajun chicken sandwich platter. It is fyah. We will even give you a free Xlarge drink of your choice because this stuff is really spicy. Also comes with a side order of your choice. Limited time only.

Crawfish Etouffee'

This is our special dish of crawfish etouffee. It is not your ordinary etouffe platter. We only offer this dish for crawfish season because we only cook it with the freshest, primest, and biggest crawfish pieces. Comes with lightly coated garlic bread.

Shrimp Boulette Platter

Our shrimp boulettes are cooked with the finest of spiced fried coating. Give this a try. The platter comes with one side order.
