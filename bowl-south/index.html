Welcome to Bowl South

This is the website of Bowl South of Houma. We are currently working on the site for news to give to our bowl south fam. Please feel free to come here and check out our services.






Frames Bar

Stop by our bar and grill. Have a few drinks at frames while you enjoy shooting on six pool tables. Watch the latest game on our massive wall projected screen and sitting in our comfortable sofas.

Serious Bowling

The best bowlers come here. Come shoot and compete in our weekly leagues on our 20 lanes. Also video games for the kids. Come have fun with us.

Pro Shop

We have the best bowling balls in the south. Stop by the pro shop to customize your bowling ball or order one from us. We are serious about bowling. This is the place. Dont go to the other guys.
